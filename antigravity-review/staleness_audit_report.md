## Conclusion & Recommendations

1. **Re-crawl Necessity**: The average sampled database staleness is low (**6.67%**), but this is highly variable depending on the retailer. Aldi has a noticeable staleness of **20.0%** due to product rotations, whereas Tesco and Iceland show **0%** staleness.
2. **Aldi Re-crawling Strategy**: Since Aldi rotates its stock and IDs frequently, re-crawling or checking using query searches is highly recommended rather than static direct link lookups.
3. **Database Health**: The base URL structures and product ID mapping remain reliable.
